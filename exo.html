Perhaps (un)surprisingly, the discovery of an exoplanet is an endeavour you can contribute to. My masters dissertation constructed a dataset primed for the discovery of new exoplanets.
Im open sourcing the dataset(s) that I constructed. NASA supplied the original data, which is also open-source. The advantage of my dataset(s) is that they've been pre-processed & labelled and are primed for machine learning. If you wish, you can download them.
If we share our research, we can hone our skill and learn this problem-space together. If we solve the problem, then we can publish the breakthrough.
I am still trying to gauge demand for this project. If there is enough, then I endeavour to detail all knowledge I have of this problem. And we'll begin from first-principles. If your curiosity is piqued, just email me a few words. x
